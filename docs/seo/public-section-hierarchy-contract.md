# Public section hierarchy contract

The public center profile body must keep the highest-value discovery content first and the lowest-priority safety and verification notes compact near the end.

Recommended order:

1. About and public fact summary
2. Location overview
3. Gallery when approved media exists
4. Services preview when relations exist
5. Doctors preview when relations exist
6. Callback or contact fallback
7. Verification and license transparency
8. Safety note

Rules:

- Do not remove required safety copy.
- Do not promote verification copy above core discovery content unless a later trust module contract replaces it.
- Do not render empty services, doctors, or gallery sections.
- Do not add ratings, booking claims, opening status, insurance claims, emergency availability, or official regulator approval claims.
- Do not change metadata, robots, sitemap eligibility, schema, provider data, or contact visibility rules.

The footer stack is a visual grouping only. It does not change indexing, content eligibility, or contact approval rules.
